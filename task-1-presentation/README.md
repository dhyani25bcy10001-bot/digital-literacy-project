# TASK 1: DIGITAL LITERACY INFOGRAPHIC
For this task, I created a digital literacy awareness infographic using a design tool like Canva. The infographic explains the concept of digital literacy and highlights its importance for students in today’s digital world. It covers key topics such as safe internet practices, useful digital tools, and maintaining a professional online presence.

The goal of the infographic is to present information in a simple, visual, and engaging way so that students can easily understand and apply it in their daily lives. The design uses icons, short text, and a clean layout to make the content more attractive and easy to read.
