# TASK 1: DIGITAL LITERACY INFOGRAPHIC
For this task, I used Canva to design a digital literacy awareness infographic. Canva was easy to use and provided various templates, icons, and design elementsthat helped me create a visually appealing and informative layout.

My infographic covered key topics such as the meaning of digital literacy, safe internet practices, and the importance of maintaining a professional online presence.I included examples like using strong passwords, avoiding suspicious links, and creating professional profiles on platforms like GitHub and LinkedIn.

The main goal of my design was to make the information simple and engaging so that students could quickly understand why digital literacy is important.I used icons, short text, and bright colours to make it visually attractive.

One challenge I faced was organizing the content in a limited space while keeping it clear and readable. However, this helped me improve my design and content summarization skills. Overall, it was an interesting experience to combine creativity with learning.
