# TASK 4: EMAIL ETIQUETTE & COMMUNICATION

Poor digital communication can lead to misunderstandings and missed opportunities. For example, if a student sends an email to a professor without a proper subject line or greeting, it may be ignored or considered unprofessional.

In this task, I learned how to write clear and formal emails with proper structure, including subject, greeting, body, and sign-off. I also created a social media checklist highlighting responsible online behaviour.

To avoid communication issues, messages should be polite, concise, and properly formatted. Using professional language and checking for errors before sending is important.

This task helped me understand the importance of maintaining professionalism in digital communication.
