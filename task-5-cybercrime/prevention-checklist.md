# Stay Safe Online – Checklist for College Students

* Use strong, unique passwords for each account and enable two-factor authentication (2FA).

* Do not click on suspicious links or download attachments from unknown emails or messages.

* Keep your phone, apps, and antivirus software updated to avoid security vulnerabilities.

* Verify information before sharing to prevent spreading misinformation.

* Limit personal details (phone number, address, college ID) shared on social media.

* **UPI Safety:** Never share your UPI PIN or OTP with anyone—even if they claim to be from the bank.

* **UPI Safety:** Always double-check the receiver’s name/ID before sending money; avoid “collect requests” from unknown users.

* Use only trusted and secure Wi-Fi networks; avoid logging into important accounts on public Wi-Fi.

* Log out from shared or public devices after use.

* Regularly check your bank statements and transaction history for any suspicious activity.
